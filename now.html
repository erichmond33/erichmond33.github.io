Research

I have nothing left to do on this until we get real data collected, so I'm temporarily not working on this.

I'm working under Dr. Jake Qualls at Astate doing research with the No Boundaries Thinking program, a collaboration with 7 out of 9 colleges on campus. Our thesis is to use a generative adversarial network (Ai model) to create the most comprehensible font for dyslexic readers.

In short, we will be giving tests to dyslexic students to collect the data our model will use. My job was to build to website that the tests will be given from and build the Ai model.
