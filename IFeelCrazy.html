Eli Richmond

I Feel Crazy

Hey. If you don't already know me, I'm Eli, just an ambitious 21 year old. This book is everything that has happened so far. Well, not everything, otherwise this book would be much longer and a lot more boring.

Life has often felt like trying climb a rockwall with people constanlty trying to yank your footing loose.

When trying to do something ambitious, the work is hard no doubt, and I expected this. But the hardest part for me so far has been pushing back against an unexpected social convention that desperatly wants to guide us into a very specific and normal life.

I hope these humble beginnings will one day inspire others in the same way other people's stories have inspired me in these first 21 years.
