Marc Randolph Former Netflix CEO on The Diary Of A CEO Podcast

This whole conversation is fascinating, and I've never heard TK's story before. There are a ton of business insights packed throughout this episode; however, two really stood out to me as novel and interesting.

1. "There is no such thing as a good idea. [...] Rather than dreaming about how amazing this idea is, the first thing you think about and the only thing you think about is how can I quickly and cheaply collide this idea with a real person and find out is it in fact a good idea or bad idea."

He goes on to tell a story about a college girl validating a clothes borrowing website; it is fantastic. Watch the clip here.

2. "Q: Hard work, does it matter? Well, since you asked it so simply, I'd say no."

The context for this answer is incredible, particularly his part two story about planes. It is seriously worth the listen for all you contrarian thinkers out there. Watch the clip here.
