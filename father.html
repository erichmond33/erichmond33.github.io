Eli Richmond

My father

Some fathers run away.
Some drink all day.
Others lie, cheat, and steal.
But mine
is good.

No doubt he isn't perfect.
But not lazy, selfish, or mean.
Good qualities to have
when raising a family.
Surprisingly these are free
yet today
seem to be a rarity.
Simply put, good character
means a lot to me.

My father didn't change the world.
He didn't build computers, cars,
or rockets for mars.
Being moral and normal
is not indifferent.
Despite what we see on the internet
a good Father
is significant.

I've never known hunger.
I've never known danger
from any person
or any stranger.
All your sacrifice
has made my life nice,
pleasant, and hopeful.
I get to dream
because you created a home.

Trust me I know
I don't say this enough.
I love you.
Thank you
for being my father.
