On Leadership...

As we get older, we realize the world is hard. We start to realize the value of a good person and how nice it is to have strong people in our lives to lean on when the going gets tough ‚Äì the kind of people that don't break down or leave.

I don't know very many men that fit this description today. I'm not sure why more strong men don't exist, but I do know the world would be better if we had more of them.

Becoming virtuous, strong, competent men is our moral duty as human beings. It won't make you famous or rich, but it will come with a deep sense of fullness that can't be found any other way.
