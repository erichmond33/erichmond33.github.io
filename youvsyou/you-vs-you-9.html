On People...

Often, life is good, and often, the byproduct of not encountering hateful, rotten people for a long period of time is the importance of fundamental being-a-good-person-ness gets somewhat forgotten.

However, when life eventually puts those kind of people in the same room as us, it becomes abundantly clear that these fundamentals are absolutely, concretely non-negotiable for life to not be miserably, awfully, and unnecessarily hard.

Be a good person and cherish the peaceful times for one day you will be face-to-face with a huge turd of a human being.
