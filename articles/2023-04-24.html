Feeling rhymers

I love consuming great art and paying attention to what it rhymes with. I ask myself, what other emotions, experiences, and ideas does this cause to bubble up to my awareness?

This is fun, but it also helps me create. Because creating is just going the other direction.

It is being able to capture a state of being in paintings, music, text, etc.

Artists are feeling rhymers.
