Smile and wave

Life is hard. Life can be lonely. But sometimes you will pass someone you know.

Often, we are too busy to acknowledge them, too caught up with my our own lives.

Instead, gather all the energy you have got, smile and wave. It is worth it.

The happiness it brings you and them is far greater than the small amount of effort it requires.

Life is always better with a little more love.

If I know you and I don't smile and wave, remind me :) I probably need a smile more than usual.
