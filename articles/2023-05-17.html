Dude face care

Every night before you go to sleep grab a towel and soak a corner of it in cold water.

Gently wash your whole face.

You can't have too much or too cold of water. So it is better to overdo it.

That's all. Forget the expensive face washes and creams. Do this without skipping.

My face is considerably clearer.
