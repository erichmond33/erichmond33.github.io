Give it all you've got

Great teams are beautiful. If you ever get the chance to be in a group working extremely hard towards a shared interest, please give it all you've got or leave.

This is one of the rarest experiences. We will only get a handful of these opportunities, if we are lucky.

Great teams are more capable than the sum of their parts. Extra care gets compounded in ways it normally wouldn't. However, lack of care compounds in the opposite direction.

We should consider caring deeply. Nobody lives too full a life. And when we choose not to care and not to leave, we are corroding something beautiful and fragile.

Nothing excites me more than these rare opportunities.
