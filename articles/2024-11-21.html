The Eli birthday gift giving policy

Policy #1: I am not expecting a gift, and you shouldn't feel pressured to bring one.

Policy #2: If you are compelled to get me a gift but are unsure what to get (gift giving is hard!), go to a thrift shop, Goodwill, etc and find the silliest, funniest, dumbest thing you can find under $10 – something super random that really cracks you up. That's the perfect gift for me. I can't wait to see what you find!

Policy #3: If you are compelled to get me a gift and do have something in mind, I'm going to love it no matter what. Some of my favorite shirts are my favorite simply because they were a gift from someone close and not because I would've picked them out myself.

Policy #4: Refer to #1. Seriously, I'm just glad to see you :)

This is weird. Why did you write this?

I hate the grey area of not really knowing whether I'm expected to bring a gift for other people's birthdays. This is meant to clear that up for the people I invite.
