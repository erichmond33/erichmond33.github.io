Compressed Lessons

Bargain with the future

The most successful people sacrifice who they are today for who they can become tomorrow.

They literally bargain with the future. Fascinating.

However, this lesson is useless without discipline.

Have higher purpose

The daily grind, no matter what you are striving for, will suck most of the time.

An inspiring mission will make the day to day struggle worthwhile.

So we must align ourselves with the stars. We must have higher purpose.

Angst? No, excitement

I need to compete. Period.

I just love it so much. It is exhilarating.

This isn't true for everyone, but I get a lot of energy from competition.

You don't know what you don't know

So learn, learn, learn.

I can't say this enough.

You are either learning, growing, and improving or you are dying.

You show me a person who doesn't actively learn, and I'll show you mindless body.

Simply, leadership

There are many nuances to leadership, but there are three rules all leaders must follow to gain the respect of their peers.

Rule #1: Work way harder than everyone else.

Rule #2: You must be an expert in the domain you are leading, or at least be in the process of working your tail off to become one.

Rule #3: You aren't too good for ANY job. Do the little things often. I.e. cleaning up, unloading, moving chairs, and things like that.

Be nice to everyone

More opportunities will come your way, and you will be more successful.

You and those around you will be happier.

You will learn so much if you genuinely listen with a good heart.

Be bored

Life gets so fast in a hurry.

We have a million obligations and no time to figure out ourselves.

Surprisingly it is really hard to do nothing. TV is much easier.

Nonetheless, do nothing for 30 minutes a day. I promise you will find ideas, forgotten dreams, and clarity.

You will also find many buried problems, but that is a good thing.
