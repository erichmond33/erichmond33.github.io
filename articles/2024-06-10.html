Shared living is great

Every single time I told a friend or family member that I would be staying in a shared-living space during my move to San Francisco, they all made a comment on how awful this living situation would probably be. They were wrong.

In retrospect, I would have hated living in SF by myself during this past month. I knew exactly one person here, and he was my landlord. The guys in the house showed me the city and plugged me into the tech scene way faster than if I had been living alone. It was so nice to come home and have people to talk to because the loneliness of moving away from everyone you know is very real.
