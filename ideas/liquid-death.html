Interesting Ideas:

Products don't exist - only brands do

Why did Liquid Death work?

  1. It is heathy - for all of the skaters and motocross guys that just wanted water not Redbull.
  2. It is cool - would you pick this or one of the other water brands?
  3. It is better for the environment - you get to feel good about not using plastic at no extra cost.

And of course they did so many things right. For example, establishing a demand before actually buying any product.
